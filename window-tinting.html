Why Tint?

Comfort you feel. Protection you don't see.

Quality window film does far more than darken glass. The right sun-control film blocks the infrared radiation that heats your cabin and the UV rays that fade your dashboard, seats and skin — all while reducing glare for a safer, cooler drive.

We fit films in a range of shades and performance grades, applied bubble-free with precision-cut patterns. (Films are fitted in line with applicable regulations — ask us about compliant options.)
