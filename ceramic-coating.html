What Is Ceramic Coating?

A permanent bond. A wet-look that lasts.

Unlike wax that washes away in weeks, a ceramic coating chemically bonds with your paint to form a semi-permanent, hydrophobic layer. The result is a deep, mirror-like gloss, dramatically easier washing, and resistance to UV fade, bird droppings, water spots and light chemical staining.

We offer ceramic and next-gen graphene coatings for paint, glass, wheels and even leather & plastic trim.

Our Process

Prep is everything

A coating locks in whatever is underneath — so we obsess over correction and decontamination first.

Decontamination Wash

Foam wash, iron-fallout removal and clay-bar to strip bonded contaminants.

Paint Correction

Machine polishing removes swirls and oxidation so the gloss is flawless.

Surface Prep

Panels are wiped down with a prep solvent for a perfectly clean bonding surface.

Coating Application

Ceramic / graphene applied by hand, leveled and inspected under detailing lights.

Curing & Handover

The coating cures in our controlled bay before careful handover and aftercare guidance.
