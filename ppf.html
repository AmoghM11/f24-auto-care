What Is PPF?

Protection you can't see. Damage that never happens.

Paint Protection Film is a transparent thermoplastic urethane layer applied over your vehicle's paint. Its self-healing top coat erases light swirls and marks with heat from the sun, while the film itself takes the hits from gravel, road debris, bug acids and car-wash micro-scratches.

At F24 we precision-cut and hand-wrap each panel for seamless, edge-wrapped coverage — on everything from daily hatchbacks to exotic supercars and superbikes.

Our Process

How we wrap your car

A meticulous, dust-controlled process — because PPF is only as good as the prep beneath it.

Inspection & Wash

We assess the paint, then perform a full decontamination wash and clay treatment.

Paint Correction (if needed)

Any swirls or marks are corrected first — film locks in a perfect surface.

Precision Cutting

Each panel is wrapped and edge-tucked for a seamless, near-invisible finish.
