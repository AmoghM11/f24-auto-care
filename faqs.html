PPF (Paint Protection Film) is a thick, self-healing urethane film that physically absorbs stone chips, scratches and swirls. Ceramic coating is a thin liquid-glass layer that adds deep gloss, hydrophobic water-beading and protection from UV and chemicals — but it doesn't stop physical impacts. Many owners combine both: PPF on high-impact areas and a coating over everything for shine and easy cleaning.

Quality PPF typically lasts several years depending on the film grade and how the car is used. Ceramic coatings range from around 1 year for entry tiers up to 5+ years for flagship and graphene coatings — provided they're maintained properly. We'll recommend the right longevity for your needs and budget.
