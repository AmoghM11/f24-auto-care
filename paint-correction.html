The Science

We remove defects — we don't hide them.

Quick "buffing" jobs often just fill swirls with glaze that washes out. True paint correction permanently levels a microscopic layer of clear coat to eliminate the defects, measured and controlled so your paint stays healthy.

  • Removes swirls, holograms & oxidation
  • Restores depth, clarity & reflection
  • Perfect prep before coating or PPF
